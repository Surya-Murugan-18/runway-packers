Popular Questions

Your quick guide to the top FAQs and everything you need to know about our services

How soon should I contact Runway Packers & Movers before my move?

Contact us at least 24 hours in advance to secure your move.

How long does a pre-move survey at my home take?

Typically 3 hours. Our Field Officer will assess, quote, and plan your move efficiently

How do I know which items I can move?

Our Field Officer will help identify items that can be safely moved and those needing special handling.

Why do I need insurance if my items are professionally packed?

Accidents can happen during transit; insurance ensures your valuables are protected.

How is insurance coverage calculated?

Coverage is based on your items’ declared value, assessed with our Field Officer’s help.

What are my responsibilities during the move?

Provide essential documents and be present during packing to identify valuable items.

What type of containers are used for transport?

We use sealed, weather-proof containers to protect your belongings during transit.

How long does packing and loading take at my residence?

About 3 hours per room; our team works efficiently while handling items with care.

Are there items that cannot be loaded onto the vehicle?

Pets, liquids, oils, inflammable, and hazardous items are not allowed; a full list is provided during the survey.

Can Runway Packers & Movers transport my car?

We provide specialized vehicles to transport your car safely and securely.
